# How to Connect Dropbox

**Seamlessly sync your existing documents to the Data Room without manual uploads.**

*Time required: 5 minutes*

---

## Why Connect Dropbox?

Integrating your Dropbox account allows you to:
- **Save Time**: Import thousands of files in one click.
- **Stay Synced**: (Optional) Keep files updated in both places.
- **Maintain Structure**: Preserve your existing folder hierarchy.

*Note: Legacy Architect OS also supports Google Drive and OneDrive. See [Connect Google Drive](connect-google-drive.md) for details.*

---

## Prerequisites

- A **Legendary** or higher plan subscription.
- An active Dropbox account.
- "Admin" or "Editor" permissions on the Dropbox folders you wish to sync.

---

## Step 1: Navigate to Integrations

1. Log in to your Legacy Architect OS dashboard.
2. Click **Settings** in the bottom left sidebar.
3. Select the **Integrations** tab.
4. Locate the **Dropbox** card under "Cloud Storage".

---

## Step 2: Authorize Connection

1. Click the **"Connect"** button on the Dropbox card.
2. A pop-up window will appear from Dropbox.
3. **Log in** to your Dropbox account if prompted.
4. Click **"Allow"** to grant Legacy Architect OS permission to view your files.
   - *Permission details*: We only request confusing "Read" access to import files. We do not delete or modify your original files.

5. The pop-up will close, and you should see a **"Connected"** status with a green checkmark.

---

## Step 3: Select Folders to Import

Once connected, you need to tell us what to grab.

1. Click **"Configure"** or **"Import Files"** on the Dropbox card.
2. You will see a file picker showing your Dropbox structure.
3. **Browse** and **Select** the folders you want to import.
   - *Best Practice*: Create a dedicated "Due Diligence" or "M&A" folder in Dropbox first, then select only that folder.
4. Click **"Import Selected"**.

---

## Step 4: Choose Import Mode

You will be asked how you want to handle these files:

### Option A: One-Time Copy (Recommended)
- Copies files from Dropbox to Legacy Architect OS.
- Disconnects after copying.
- **Pros**: Safe; changes in Data Room don't affect your personal Dropbox.
- **Cons**: Updates in Dropbox won't auto-sync.

### Option B: Two-Way Sync
- Keeps files identical in both places.
- **Pros**: Update a file on your desktop, and it updates the Data Room.
- **Cons**: Deleting a file in one place might delete it in the other (depending on settings).

**Select your preference and click "Start Import".**

---

## Step 5: Verify Your Data Room

1. Navigate to the **Data Room** module.
2. Confirm that your folders have appeared.
   - *Note: Large imports may take 5-10 minutes to process.*
3. Check a few random files to ensure they open correctly.

---

## Troubleshooting

### "Connection Failed" Error
- **Browser Pop-ups**: Ensure your browser isn't blocking the Dropbox login pop-up.
- **Business Accounts**: If you use Dropbox Business, your team admin may need to approve the app.

### Files Not Showing Up
- **File Types**: We support PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, JPG, and PNG. Proprietary or system files may be skipped.
- **Size Limits**: Ensure no single file exceeds 2GB.
