# How to Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

**Secure your account and protect sensitive business data with an extra layer of security.**

*Time required: 5 minutes*

---

## Why 2FA Matters

**Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** requires two forms of identification to access your account:
1. Your password (something you know)
2. A code from your phone (something you have)

**Importance**:
- **Data Protection**: protects sensitive financial and legal documents in your Data Room.
- **Compliance**: Often required by buyers and auditors for secure data sharing.
- **Peace of Mind**: Prevents unauthorized access even if your password is stolen.

---

## Step 1: Install an Authenticator App

We recommend using a dedicated authenticator app for the most secure experience.

**Popular Apps**:
- **Google Authenticator** ([iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/google-authenticator/id388497605) | [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.authenticator2))
- **Microsoft Authenticator** ([iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/microsoft-authenticator/id983156458) | [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.azure.authenticator))
- **Authy** ([iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/twilio-authy/id494168017) | [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.authy.authy))

*Note: SMS (text message) authentication is also available but is considered less secure than an app.*

---

## Step 2: Navigate to Security Settings

1. Log in to your Legacy Architect OS account.
2. Click your **Profile Avatar** in the top right corner.
3. Select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
4. Click the **Security** tab in the left sidebar.

---

## Step 3: Enable 2FA

1. Locate the **"Two-Factor Authentication"** section.
2. Toggle the switch to **On**.
3. A setup modal will appear with a **QR Code**.

---

## Step 4: Link Your Authenticator App

1. Open your Authenticator App on your phone.
2. Tap the **"+"** or **"Add Account"** button.
3. Select **"Scan QR Code"**.
4. Point your phone's camera at the QR code on your screen.
5. The app will generate a 6-digit code.

---

## Step 5: Verify and Finalize

1. Enter the **6-digit code** from your app into the field on your screen.
2. Click **"Verify & Enable"**.
3. **Backup Codes**: You will be shown a list of backup codes.
   - **IMPORTANT**: Download or print these codes and store them in a safe place.
   - Use these if you lose your phone or cannot access your app.

---

## Logging In with 2FA

Next time you log in:
1. Enter your email and password.
2. You will be prompted for a 2FA code.
3. Open your Authenticator App to get the current code.
4. Enter the code to access your account.

---

## Troubleshooting

### Lost Phone / Can't Access App
1. Click **"Try another method"** on the login screen.
2. Select **"Backup codes"**.
3. Enter one of the backup codes you saved during setup.

### Code Not Working
- **Time Sync**: Ensure your phone's time is set to "Automatic." If the time is off by even a minute, the codes will be invalid.
- **Refresh**: Wait for the code to change (usually every 30 seconds) and try the new one.

### Disable 2FA
*Not recommended unless necessary.*
1. Go to **Settings > Security**.
2. Toggle 2FA to **Off**.
3. You will need to enter a final code to confirm disabling.
