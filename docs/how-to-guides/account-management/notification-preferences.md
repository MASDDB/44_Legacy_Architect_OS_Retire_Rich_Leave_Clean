# How to Configure Notification Preferences

**Stay informed about critical events without getting overwhelmed by emails.**

*Time required: 3 minutes*

---

## Why Customize Notifications?

The Legacy Architect OS sends alerts for various activities. Customizing these ensures you see what's important:

- **Security Alerts**: Immediate login warnings (Critical)
- **Data Room Activity**: Know when a buyer views a file (High Priority)
- **Task Reminders**: Keep your exit planning on schedule (Medium Priority)
- **Newsletter**: Tips and market updates (Low Priority)

---

## Step 1: Access Notification Settings

1. Log in to your account.
2. Click your **Profile Avatar** in the top right.
3. Select **Settings**.
4. Click the **Notifications** tab in the sidebar.

---

## Step 2: Configure Email Preferences

Manage which emails land in your inbox.

### Critical Alerts (Locked)
*These cannot be disabled for security reasons.*
- Password resets
- New device logins
- Billing failures

### Activity Alerts
- [ ] **Data Room Views**: Notify me when someone views a document.
  - *Recommended: **Immediate** during active due diligence.*
- [ ] **Data Room Downloads**: Notify me when someone downloads a file.
  - *Recommended: **Immediate***
- [ ] **New Tasks**: Notify me when a task is assigned to me.

### Digest Emails
- [ ] **Weekly Summary**: A recap of all activity and task progress.
- [ ] **Monthly Report**: High-level overview of Exit Readiness Score changes.

---

## Step 3: Configure In-App Notifications

These alerts appear solely within the application (bell icon).

- **Mentions**: When an advisor tags you in a comment.
- **Task Due Dates**: Reminder 24 hours before a task is due.
- **System Updates**: New feature announcements.

**To adjust:**
1. Scroll to **"In-App Notifications"**.
2. Toggle switches **On** or **Off** based on preference.

---

## Step 4: Manage Advisor & Team Notifications

If you are an Admin, you can set defaults for your team.

1. Go to **Organization Settings** > **Notifications**.
2. **Default Policy**: Choose what team members receive by default.
3. **Advisor Access**: Control if advisors get emails when you upload files.

---

## Best Practices for Deal Mode

When you are actively selling your business ("Deal Mode"), change your settings to:

| Notification Type | Recommended Setting | Why? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Data Room Access** | **Immediate Email** | Know instantly if a buyer is interested. |
| **Q&A New Question** | **Immediate Email** | Response speed builds trust. |
| **Weekly Summary** | **On** | Keep a record of weekly progress. |
| **Marketing Emails** | **Off** | Reduce noise to focus on the deal. |

---

## Troubleshooting

### Not Receiving Emails?
1. Check your **Spam/Junk** folder.
2. Verify your email address in **Profile Settings**.
3. Ensure `notifications@legacyarchitectOS.com` is whitelisted.

### Too Many Emails?
1. Switch **Data Room Activity** from "Immediate" to "Daily Digest".
2. Turn off "Marketing & Tips".
