# How to Create and Verify Your Account

**Learn how to sign up for Legacy Architect OS and verify your email.**

*Time required: 5 minutes*

---

## Prerequisites

- Valid email address
- Internet connection
- Supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)

---

## Step 1: Navigate to Sign Up Page

1. Go to [https://legacyarchitectos.com](https://legacyarchitectos.com)
2. Click the **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"** button
   - Located in the top right corner of the homepage
   - Or in the center of the page

---

## Step 2: Enter Your Information

Fill out the registration form:

### Required Fields

**Email Address**
- Use your business email (recommended)
- This will be your username
- Example: `john@yourbusiness.com`

**Password**
- Minimum 8 characters
- Must include:
  - At least one uppercase letter
  - At least one lowercase letter
  - At least one number
- Recommended: 12+ characters with special characters

**Full Name**
- Enter your first and last name
- This appears in your profile and communications

### Optional Fields

**Company Name** (can be added later)
**Phone Number** (can be added later)

---

## Step 3: Accept Terms and Create Account

1. Review the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
   - Click the links to read full documents
2. Check the box: **"I agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy"**
3. Click **"Create Account"** button

### What Happens Next

- Your account is created immediately
- You're redirected to a "Verify Email" page
- A verification email is sent to your inbox

---

## Step 4: Verify Your Email

### Check Your Inbox

1. Open your email application
2. Look for an email from `noreply@legacyarchitectos.com`
   - Subject: "Verify your Legacy Architect OS account"
   - Should arrive within 1-2 minutes

### If You Don't See the Email

**Check Spam/Junk Folder**
- Sometimes verification emails are filtered
- Mark as "Not Spam" if found there

**Check Promotions Tab** (Gmail users)
- Gmail may categorize it as promotional
- Drag to Primary inbox

**Wait a Few Minutes**
- Email delivery can take up to 5 minutes
- Refresh your inbox

### Click the Verification Link

1. Open the verification email
2. Click the **"Verify Email Address"** button
   - Or copy/paste the verification link into your browser
3. You'll be redirected to Legacy Architect OS
4. You'll see a confirmation message: "Email verified successfully!"

---

## Step 5: Complete Your Profile

After verification, you'll be prompted to complete your profile:

### Choose Your Role

Select the option that best describes you:

- **Business Owner** - You own or co-own a business
- **M&A Advisor/Consultant** - You help businesses with exits
- **Internal Team Member** - You work for a business owner (accountant, legal, operations)
- **Other** - None of the above

### Set Your Timezone

- Select your timezone from the dropdown
- This ensures correct timestamps for notifications and reports
- Example: `(GMT-05:00) Eastern Time (US & Canada)`

### Upload Profile Photo (Optional)

- Click **"Upload Photo"**
- Select an image file (JPG, PNG)
- Maximum size: 5MB
- Recommended: Professional headshot

### Click "Continue"

- Your profile is saved
- You're redirected to the dashboard

---

## Step 6: Explore Your Dashboard

You're now logged in! You'll see:

- **Welcome message** with your name
- **Exit Readiness Score** (0 until you take assessment)
- **Quick actions** (Take Assessment, Upload Documents, etc.)
- **Navigation menu** (left sidebar)

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Email already exists"

**Cause**: You've already created an account with this email

**Solution**:
1. Click **"Log In"** instead of Sign Up
2. If you forgot your password, click **"Forgot Password?"**
3. Follow the password reset process

### Problem: Verification email not received

**Solutions**:
1. **Wait 5 minutes** - Email can be delayed
2. **Check spam/junk folder**
3. **Check email address** - Make sure you entered it correctly
4. **Resend verification email**:
   - Log in to your account
   - You'll see a banner: "Email not verified"
   - Click **"Resend Verification Email"**

### Problem: Verification link expired

**Cause**: Verification links expire after 24 hours

**Solution**:
1. Log in to your account
2. Click **"Resend Verification Email"**
3. Check your inbox for new email
4. Click the new verification link

### Problem: Can't log in after verification

**Solutions**:
1. **Clear browser cache and cookies**
   - Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data
   - Firefox: Options → Privacy → Clear Data
2. **Try a different browser**
3. **Reset your password**:
   - Click **"Forgot Password?"** on login page
   - Follow password reset process
4. **Contact support**: support@legacyarchitectos.com

## Security Best Practices

### Use a Strong Password

**Good password example**:
- `MyBusiness2025!ExitPlan`
- 23 characters, mix of upper/lower/numbers/symbols

**Bad password example**:
- `password123`
- Too common, easily guessed

### Enable Two-Factor Authentication (Recommended)

After creating your account:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **"Enable Two-Factor Authentication"**
3. Follow the setup process

See: [How to Set Up Two-Factor Authentication](two-factor-authentication.md)

### Don't Share Your Password

- Never share your password with anyone
- Legacy Architect OS staff will never ask for your password
- Be wary of phishing emails
