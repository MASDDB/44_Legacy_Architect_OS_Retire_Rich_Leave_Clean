# How to Update Your Business Profile

**Keep your business information current and accurate.**

*Time required: 10 minutes*

---

## Why Update Your Business Profile?

Your business profile:
- Personalizes your experience and recommendations
- Provides context for your Exit Readiness Score
- Helps advisors understand your business
- Tracks your exit goals and timeline

---

## Accessing Your Business Profile

### Method 1: From Dashboard

1. Log in to Legacy Architect OS
2. Click your name/avatar (top right corner)
3. Select **"Business Profile"** from dropdown

### Method 2: From Settings

1. Click **"Settings"** in left sidebar
2. Click **"Business Profile"** tab

---

## Basic Information

### Business Name

**What to enter**: Your official business name
- Example: "Smith & Associates LLC"
- This appears in reports and communications

**To update**:
1. Click in the **"Business Name"** field
2. Type your business name
3. Changes save automatically

### Legal Entity Name (if different)

**What to enter**: Your legal entity name if different from business name
- Example: Business Name: "Joe's Pizza"
- Legal Entity: "Joseph's Pizza Holdings LLC"

**When to use**: If your DBA (Doing Business As) differs from legal name

### Industry

**What to select**: Your primary industry
1. Click the **"Industry"** dropdown
2. Select from list (e.g., "Professional Services", "Manufacturing", "Retail")
3. If your industry isn't listed, select "Other" and specify

**Why it matters**: Industry affects:
- Valuation multiples
- Benchmark comparisons
- Relevant recommendations

### Sub-Industry (Optional)

**What to enter**: More specific industry classification
- Example: Industry = "Professional Services"
- Sub-Industry = "Accounting & Tax Services"

---

## Contact Information

### Business Address

**What to enter**: Your primary business location

**Fields**:
- Street Address
- City
- State/Province
- ZIP/Postal Code
- Country

**Why it matters**: Used for:
- Tax jurisdiction
- Market analysis
- Buyer targeting (if applicable)

### Business Phone

**What to enter**: Main business phone number
- Format: (555) 123-4567
- Include area code

### Business Email

**What to enter**: General business email
- Example: info@yourbusiness.com
- Different from your login email (optional)

### Website

**What to enter**: Your business website URL
- Include https://
- Example: https://www.yourbusiness.com

---

## Financial Overview

### Annual Revenue

**What to enter**: Most recent full year revenue
- Enter as a number (e.g., 2500000 for $2.5M)
- Don't include dollar signs or commas
- System formats it automatically

**When to update**: After each fiscal year end

### Number of Employees

**What to enter**: Full-time equivalent (FTE) count
- Include full-time employees
- Count part-time as 0.5 FTE each
- Include yourself as owner
- Example: 3 full-time + 2 part-time = 4 FTE

### Year Founded

**What to enter**: Year business was established
- Format: YYYY (e.g., 2015)
- Use year of incorporation or first revenue

---

## Ownership Structure

### Ownership Type

**Select one**:
- **Sole Proprietorship**: Single owner, no formal entity
- **Partnership**: Multiple owners, partnership agreement
- **LLC**: Limited Liability Company
- **S-Corporation**: S-Corp tax election
- **C-Corporation**: Standard corporation
- **Other**: Specify in notes

### Ownership Percentage

**What to enter**: Your ownership stake
- Enter as percentage (e.g., 100, 75, 50)
- If multiple owners, enter your share

### Number of Owners

**What to enter**: Total number of owners/shareholders
- Include yourself
- Example: You + 1 partner = 2

---

## Exit Goals

### Target Exit Timeline

**Select one**:
- **1-2 years**: Actively preparing for sale
- **3-5 years**: Building value for eventual exit
- **5+ years**: Long-term value building
- **Not planning to exit**: Building for legacy/family succession
- **Undecided**: Still exploring options

**Why it matters**: Determines:
- Recommended priorities
- Mission suggestions
- Assessment focus areas

### Desired Sale Price Range

**What to enter**: Your target sale price (optional)
- Low end: Minimum acceptable price
- High end: Ideal/aspirational price
- Example: $3M - $5M

**Note**: This is private and used only for:
- Valuation gap analysis
- Goal tracking
- Personalized recommendations

### Post-Exit Plans

**Select all that apply**:
- **Retire completely**: Done working
- **Start new business**: Serial entrepreneur
- **Consult/advise**: Stay involved part-time
- **Pursue passion project**: Non-business goals
- **Undecided**: Still figuring it out

---

## Additional Information

### Business Description

**What to enter**: Brief description of what your business does
- 2-3 sentences
- Focus on what you do and who you serve
- Example: "We provide commercial HVAC installation and maintenance to office buildings and retail centers in the greater Chicago area. Our clients range from small strip malls to Fortune 500 corporate campuses."

**Character limit**: 500 characters

### Unique Selling Proposition (USP)

**What to enter**: What makes your business different/better
- 1-2 sentences
- Focus on competitive advantages
- Example: "24/7 emergency service with 2-hour response time and proprietary preventive maintenance system that reduces client downtime by 40%."

### Key Challenges

**What to enter**: Top 3 challenges facing your business
- One per line
- Be honest (this helps with recommendations)
- Example:
  - "Heavy owner dependency - I'm involved in all major decisions"
  - "Customer concentration - top 3 clients are 60% of revenue"
  - "Inconsistent cash flow - seasonal business"

---

## Saving Your Changes

### Auto-Save

Most fields save automatically when you:
- Click out of the field
- Press Tab to move to next field
- Select an option from dropdown

**Look for**: Green checkmark or "Saved" indicator

### Manual Save

Some sections require clicking **"Save Changes"** button:
- Business Description
- Key Challenges
- Exit Goals section

**Always click "Save Changes" before leaving the page**

---

## Updating Your Profile Regularly

### When to Update

**Quarterly** (recommended):
- Review all information for accuracy
- Update revenue if significantly changed
- Adjust exit timeline if plans change

**Annually** (minimum):
- Update annual revenue (after fiscal year end)
- Update number of employees
- Review and update exit goals

**As needed**:
- Business name or legal entity changes
- Address changes
- Ownership structure changes
- Major business changes

### Why Regular Updates Matter

- **Accurate recommendations**: System uses current data
- **Better benchmarking**: Compare to similar businesses
- **Progress tracking**: See how you're improving over time
- **Buyer readiness**: Current information for due diligence

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Changes not saving

**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Look for error message (usually red text)
3. Try refreshing page and re-entering
4. Clear browser cache
5. Try different browser

### Problem: Can't find a field

**Solution**: Some fields only appear based on previous selections
- Example: "Legal Entity Name" only shows if different from Business Name
- Check if you need to select/enable something first

### Problem: Don't know what to enter

**Solutions**:
1. Hover over field label for tooltip help
2. Click the (?) icon if available
3. Leave optional fields blank
4. Contact support: support@legacyarchitectos.com

---

## Privacy & Security

### Who Can See Your Profile?

**Only you** can see your full business profile, unless:
- You invite team members (they see relevant sections)
- You share with advisors (you control what they see)
- You share data room with buyers (you control what's included)

### What's Never Shared

- Desired sale price
- Key challenges
- Personal notes
- Financial details (unless you explicitly share)
