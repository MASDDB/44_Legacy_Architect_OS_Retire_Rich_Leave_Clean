# The Advisor Playbook

**A guide for M&A Advisors, Brokers, and Value Creation Consultants using Legacy Architect OS.**

---

## Overview

Legacy Architect OS is designed to be collaborative. As an advisor, you play a critical role in guiding the business owner through the exit journey. This guide explains how to manage your clients effectively on the platform.

---

## 1. Client Management

### Onboarding a New Client
1. **Invite**: The client must invite you as an **Admin** or **Advisor** to their Organization.
2. **Accept**: Check your email for the invitation link.
3. **Switch Profiles**: Use the "Organization Switcher" in the top-left corner to toggle between your different client accounts.

### Roles & Permissions
- **Admin**: Full access. Can invite others, change billing, and manage all settings. (Recommended for primary Advisors).
- **Collaborator**: Can upload/edit files but cannot change global settings. (Good for junior analysts).
- **Viewer**: Read-only. (Good for passive observers).

---

## 2. The Assessment Phase

Your first job is to baseline the business.

1. **Run the Assessment**: Walk through the Exit Readiness Assessment *with* the client.
2. **Review the Gap Report**: Identify the red flags.
3. **Set the Initial Strategy**: Choose 3 high-impact Missions to tackle first.

**Pro Tip**: Use the "Benchmarking" feature to show the client how they compare to peers in their industry (if available on their plan).

---

## 3. Building the Data Room

Advisors often do the heavy lifting here.

### Structure
- Use the standard Legacy Architect OS template.
- **Do not** create custom folder structures unless necessary—buyers appreciate consistency.

### The "Teaser" vs. "CIM"
- Create a dedicated folder called `0. Marketing Materials`.
- **Teaser**: Public-facing, no NDA required.
- **CIM**: Restricted access, NDA required.

---

## 4. Managing Diligence (Deal Mode)

When buyers are active, you are the gatekeeper.

### Vetting Buyers
1. **Add Buyer**: Invite the buyer to the Data Room.
2. **Set Permissions**: Start with "View Only" to the "Teaser" folder.
3. **Expand Access**: Once NDA is signed, add them to the "CIM" group.
4. **Full Access**: Only grant access to sensitive financials (customer lists, granular P&L) after an LOI is signed.

### Q&A Management
- Use the platform's **Q&A Module** instead of email.
- **Why?** It creates a legal audit trail of exactly what was disclosed and when.

---

## 5. Value Audit

Before going to market, conduct your own audit:
1. **Financials**: Check for EBITDA add-backs that aren't documented.
2. **Contracts**: Ensure all key employee agreements are signed and scanned.
3. **IP**: Verify trademark statuses.
---

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't see my client's account"
- Ensure they invited you to the correct email address.
- Ask them to check **Settings > Team** to verify your status is "Active".

### "How do I export data for my records?"
- Use the **Admin Export** feature in **Settings > Data** to download a full archive of the Data Room and Activity Logs for your firm's compliance records.
