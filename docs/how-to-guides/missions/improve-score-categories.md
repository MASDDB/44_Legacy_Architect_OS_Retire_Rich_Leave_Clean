# How to Improve Your Score Categories

**Strategic actions to move your "Weak" areas to "Strong" and maximize your exit value.**

*Time required: 15-30 minutes per week*

---

## Understanding Score Categories

Your Exit Readiness Score is an aggregate of 4 key categories. Buyers look for balance; a great product (Operations) doesn't outweigh messy books (Financials).

**The 4 Pillars**:
1. **Financials**: The health and verifiable history of your numbers.
2. **Operations**: Your ability to deliver without the owner.
3. **Legal/Risk**: Protection against lawsuits and liabilities.
4. **Market**: Your competitive position and growth potential.

---

## Pillar 1: Financials ("The Truth")

**Goal**: Clean, verifiable, and growing numbers.

| Status | Symptom | Fix It |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 🔴 **Weak** | P&L and Personal tax returns mixed. | **Separate** personal expenses. Get a dedicated business bank account. |
| 🟡 **Fair** | Cash basis accounting only. | **Convert** to Accrual basis (GAAP). Track KPIs monthly. |
| 🟢 **Strong** | Audited financials, clear growth. | **Conduct** a "Quality of Earnings" (QofE) study for upcoming diligence. |

*Related Mission: [Launch Cash-Boost Campaign](../missions/launch-cash-boost.md)*

---

## Pillar 2: Operations ("The Engine")

**Goal**: The business runs better *without* you.

| Status | Symptom | Fix It |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 🔴 **Weak** | "I'm the only one who knows how to..." | **Document** your daily tasks. Delegate 1 item this week. |
| 🟡 **Fair** | Some processes documented, key man risk. | **Cross-train** employees. Create an Ops Manual. |
| 🟢 **Strong** | Management team runs day-to-day. | **Incentivize** key managers with a "Stay Bonus" for after the sale. |

*Related Guide: [Connect Dropbox](../data-room/connect-dropbox.md) (Centralize your SOPs)*

---

## Pillar 3: Legal/Risk ("The Shield")

**Goal**: No surprises that kill the deal.

| Status | Symptom | Fix It |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 🔴 **Weak** | Verbal agreements, no IP protection. | **Sign** written contracts with all top customers and vendors. |
| 🟡 **Fair** | Standard contracts, some exposure. | **Audit** HR files for compliance. Register trademarks. |
| 🟢 **Strong** | Clean litigation history, protected IP. | **Review** insurance policies for "Tail Coverage" post-sale. |

*Related Guide: [Share with Advisors](../data-room/share-with-advisors.md) (Get a legal audit)*

---

## Pillar 4: Market ("The Story")

**Goal**: A growing future the buyer wants to buy.

| Status | Symptom | Fix It |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 🔴 **Weak** | Declining sales, one big customer (>30%). | **Diversify**. Hunt new customers immediately. |
| 🟡 **Fair** | Flat growth, decent brand. | **Launch** a new marketing channel. Gather case studies/testimonials. |
| 🟢 **Strong** | High recurring revenue, growing market. | **Document** the "Growth Story" or "Blue Sky" plan for the buyer. |

*Related Mission: [Track Mission ROI](../missions/track-roi.md)*

---

## Building Your Action Plan

Don't try to fix everything at once.

1. **Prioritize Weakest Links**: A "Weak" score in Legal is a bigger deal-killer than a "Fair" score in Marketing.
2. **Assign Owners**: If you have a team, assign the "Operations" fix to your COO/Manager.
3. **Set Deadlines**: "We will have all customer contracts signed by Q3."
