# How to Retake the Exit Readiness Assessment

**Update your score to reflect recent improvements and track your journey to a lucrative exit.**

*Time required: 15-20 minutes*

---

## Why Retake It?

Your business is not static. As you complete Missions and improve your operations, your Exit Readiness Score should climb.
- **Track Progress**: See the direct impact of your hard work.
- **Identify New Gaps**: As you fix one area, another may become the priority.
- **Motivate the Team**: Share score improvements to build momentum.

*Recommendation: Retake the assessment every 90 days or after completing a major Mission.*

---

## Step 1: Navigate to Assessment

1. Log in to your Dashboard.
2. Click **"Assessment"** in the left sidebar.
3. You will see your current score and a history chart.

---

## Step 2: Start a New Assessment

1. Click the **"Retake Assessment"** button in the top right.
2. **Review Previous Answers**: Your previous answers are pre-filled to save time.
3. **Update Changes**: Only change the answers for areas you have improved.
   - *Example*: If you recently implemented an employment contract for key staff, change that answer from "No" to "Yes".

---

## Step 3: Be Honest

It is tempting to inflate your answers to see a higher score, but this hurts you in the long run.
- **Buyer Perspective**: Answer as if a skeptical buyer is asking.
- **Verification**: If you say "Yes" to "Do you have audited financials?", ensure you actually have the PDF to prove it.

---

## Step 4: Submit and Compare

1. Click **"Submit Assessment"**.
2. **Instant Analysis**: You will immediately see your new score.
3. **Change Report**: The system highlights:
   - **Score Increase**: "+12 points since last quarter"
   - **Category Improvements**: "Financials moved from 'Weak' to 'Fair'"

---

## Step 5: Update Your Action Plan

Your new score will generate a fresh set of recommendations.
1. Go to the **"Missions"** tab.
2. Archive completed missions.
3. Select new "Suggested Missions" based on your updated weak spots.
