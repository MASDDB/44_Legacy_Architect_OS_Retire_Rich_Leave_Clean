# How to Launch a Cash-Boost Campaign

**Generate revenue in 30 days with a proven campaign framework.**

*Time required: 1 week to launch, 30 days to complete*

---

## What is a Cash-Boost Campaign?

A focused, time-bound initiative to generate revenue from:
- Dormant customers (Reactivation)
- Active customers (Upsell)
- Happy customers (Referral)

**Typical ROI**: 3-10x investment  
**Time to results**: 7-30 days

See: [Running Your First Cash-Boost Mission](../../tutorials/first-cash-boost.md) for detailed overview

---

## Before You Start

### Choose Your Campaign Type

**Reactivation**: Best if you have 100+ customers who haven't bought in 6+ months  
**Upsell**: Best if you have tiered offerings or add-on services  
**Referral**: Best if you have high customer satisfaction (NPS 8+)

### Gather Required Information

- Customer list with contact information
- Purchase history
- Customer segments (if applicable)
- Email/outreach templates

---

## Step 1: Navigate to Missions

1. Log in to Legacy Architect OS
2. Click **"Missions"** in left sidebar
3. Click **"Cash-Boost"** tab
4. Click **"Create New Campaign"** button

---

## Step 2: Select Campaign Type

Choose one:
- **Reactivation Campaign** - Win back dormant customers
- **Upsell Campaign** - Increase spending from active customers
- **Referral Campaign** - Generate new leads from happy customers

Click **"Next"**

---

## Step 3: Name Your Campaign

**Campaign Name**: Descriptive name for tracking
- Example: "Q4 2024 Reactivation - HVAC Customers"
- Example: "Premium Upgrade Offer - December 2024"

**Campaign Goal**: What you want to achieve
- Example: "$25,000 in revenue"
- Example: "50 reactivated customers"
- Example: "20 new referrals"

Click **"Next"**

---

## Step 4: Define Your Target List

### Upload Customer List

**Option A: Upload CSV**
1. Click **"Upload CSV"**
2. Select file with customer data
3. Map columns (Name, Email, Phone, Last Purchase Date, etc.)
4. Click **"Import"**

**Option B: Select from CRM**
1. Click **"Select from CRM"**
2. Filter customers:
   - Last purchase date
   - Total spend
   - Customer status
3. Click **"Add to Campaign"**

**Option C: Manual Entry**
1. Click **"Add Manually"**
2. Enter customer details one by one
3. Click **"Save and Add Another"**

### Review Target List

- **List size**: Number of customers
- **Segments**: Any groupings (optional)
- **Exclusions**: Remove customers you don't want to contact

Click **"Next"**

---

## Step 5: Create Your Offer

### Reactivation Offer Examples

- "We miss you! 30% off your next service"
- "$99 special for returning customers (regularly $299)"
- "Come back bonus: Free [product/service] with purchase"

### Upsell Offer Examples

- "Upgrade to Premium - First month 50% off"
- "Add [service] for only $X (save 25%)"
- "Bundle and save: Get all 3 for price of 2"

### Referral Offer Examples

- "$500 credit for each referral that becomes a customer"
- "Refer 3 friends, get [service] free"
- "You get 20%, they get 20%"

### Offer Details

**Discount/Incentive**: What are you offering?
**Value**: Dollar amount or percentage
**Expiration Date**: Create urgency (7-14 days recommended)
**Terms & Conditions**: Any restrictions

Click **"Next"**

---

## Step 6: Write Your Outreach

### Email Template

The platform provides templates. Customize:

**Subject Line**:
- Keep under 50 characters
- Create curiosity or urgency
- Personalize with {FirstName}

**Body**:
- Greeting with {FirstName}
- Why you're reaching out
- The offer (clear and compelling)
- Call to action (button or link)
- Expiration date

**Example**:
```
Subject: {FirstName}, we miss you!

Hi {FirstName},

It's been a while since we've had the chance to serve you at {CompanyName}.

We've made some improvements and want to welcome you back with a special offer:

[30% OFF YOUR NEXT SERVICE]

This offer expires {ExpirationDate}, so don't wait!

Ready to give us another try?

[BOOK NOW]

Best,
{YourName}
```

### SMS Template (Optional)

If using SMS:
- Keep under 160 characters
- Include link
- Clear call to action

**Example**:
```
Hi {FirstName}! We miss you at {Company}. Come back and get 30% off - expires {Date}. Book now: {Link}
```

Click **"Next"**

---

## Step 7: Set Campaign Schedule

### Send Date & Time

**When to send**:
- **Day**: Tuesday-Thursday (best response rates)
- **Time**: 10am-2pm local time (highest open rates)
- **Avoid**: Mondays, Fridays, weekends

**Batch sending** (recommended):
- Send in batches of 100-200
- Space 1-2 hours apart
- Prevents spam filters

### Follow-Up Sequence

**Recommended sequence**:
- **Day 0**: Initial email
- **Day 3**: Follow-up to non-openers
- **Day 7**: Final reminder to non-responders

Check boxes for follow-ups you want to enable

Click **"Next"**

---

## Step 8: Set Up Tracking

### What to Track

**Email Metrics** (auto-tracked):
- Open rate
- Click rate
- Bounce rate
- Unsubscribe rate

**Response Metrics** (you track):
- Appointments scheduled
- Quotes requested
- Questions asked

**Conversion Metrics** (you track):
- Sales closed
- Revenue generated
- Average order value

### Tracking Links

The platform automatically creates tracking links:
- Each customer gets unique link
- Clicks are tracked automatically
- Conversions tracked when you mark sales

Click **"Next"**

---

## Step 9: Review and Launch

### Pre-Launch Checklist

- [ ] Target list reviewed (correct customers)
- [ ] Offer is compelling and clear
- [ ] Email/SMS copy is error-free
- [ ] Links work correctly
- [ ] Send schedule is set
- [ ] Tracking is configured

### Test Send

**Highly recommended**:
1. Click **"Send Test"**
2. Enter your email
3. Review test email
4. Check formatting, links, personalization
5. Make any needed edits

### Launch Campaign

1. Click **"Launch Campaign"**
2. Confirm launch
3. Campaign status changes to "Active"
4. First batch sends at scheduled time

---

## Managing Your Campaign

### Monitor Daily

**Week 1**:
- Check open rates (target: 25-40%)
- Check click rates (target: 5-15%)
- Respond to inquiries within 4 hours
- Schedule appointments

**Week 2-3**:
- Send follow-up emails
- Call interested prospects
- Close sales
- Update tracking

**Week 4**:
- Final push on fence-sitters
- Close remaining deals
- Calculate final ROI

### Update Tracking

As responses come in:

1. Navigate to campaign dashboard
2. Click **"Update Results"**
3. Enter:
   - Appointments scheduled
   - Sales closed
   - Revenue generated
4. Click **"Save"**

System calculates ROI automatically

## Optimizing Your Campaign

### If Open Rate is Low (<15%)

**Problem**: Subject line not compelling

**Solutions**:
- Test different subject lines
- Add personalization
- Create more urgency
- A/B test next campaign

### If Click Rate is Low (<3%)

**Problem**: Offer not compelling or unclear

**Solutions**:
- Strengthen offer
- Make CTA more prominent
- Simplify message
- Add social proof

### If Response Rate is Low (<5%)

**Problem**: Wrong audience or timing

**Solutions**:
- Segment list differently
- Try different time/day
- Adjust offer
- Follow up more aggressively

## After Campaign Completes

### Calculate Final Results

The platform shows:
- **Total sent**: Number of emails/SMS sent
- **Open rate**: % who opened
- **Click rate**: % who clicked
- **Response rate**: % who responded
- **Conversion rate**: % who bought
- **Revenue**: Total sales generated
- **Cost**: Time + materials
- **ROI**: Revenue ÷ Cost

### Document for Buyers

1. Click **"Generate Report"**
2. Download PDF summary
3. Save to Data Room (Folder 5: Sales & Marketing)

**What to include**:
- Campaign overview
- Results and ROI
- Key learnings
- How you'd scale it

### Plan Next Campaign

**If ROI was 3x+**:
- Run same campaign quarterly
- Expand to larger list
- Test second campaign type

**If ROI was 1-3x**:
- Analyze what worked/didn't
- Improve offer or targeting
- Try again with adjustments

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: High bounce rate (>5%)

**Solutions**:
- Clean your email list
- Remove old/invalid emails
- Use email verification service

### Problem: High unsubscribe rate (>1%)

**Solutions**:
- Review your messaging (too aggressive?)
- Ensure you're targeting right audience
- Reduce send frequency

### Problem: Low response despite good open/click rates

**Solutions**:
- Offer may not be compelling enough
- Follow-up may be needed
- Call interested prospects directly
