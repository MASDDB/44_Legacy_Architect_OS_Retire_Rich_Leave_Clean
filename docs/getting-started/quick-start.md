## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Sign Up

1. Go to [https://legacyarchitectos.com](https://legacyarchitectos.com)
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"**
3. Enter your information:
   - **Email address** (use your business email)
   - **Password** (minimum 8 characters)
   - **Full name**
4. Click **"Create Account"**

> **💡 Tip**: Use a strong, unique password. We recommend a password manager like 1Password or LastPass.

### Step 2: Verify Your Email

1. Check your inbox for a verification email from Legacy Architect OS
2. Click the **"Verify Email"** link
3. You'll be redirected to the platform

> **⚠️ Note**: If you don't see the email, check your spam folder. The email comes from `noreply@legacyarchitectos.com`

### Step 3: Complete Your Profile

After verification, you'll be prompted to:

1. **Choose your role**:
   - Business Owner
   - M&A Advisor/Consultant
   - Internal Team Member (Accountant, Legal, etc.)

2. **Set your timezone** (for scheduling and notifications)

3. **Optional**: Upload a profile photo

Click **"Continue"** when done.

## Exit Readiness Assessment

The assessment is the foundation of your exit journey. It evaluates your business across three critical dimensions.

### Starting the Assessment

1. From the dashboard, click **"Take Assessment"**
2. You'll see an overview of the assessment sections
3. Click **"Begin Assessment"**

### Assessment Sections

#### Section 1: Business Basics (5 minutes)
- Business name and industry
- Years in operation
- Annual revenue
- Number of employees
- Legal structure

#### Section 2: Financial Metrics (10 minutes)
- Revenue growth rate
- Gross margin
- EBITDA margin
- Customer concentration
- Recurring revenue percentage
- Cash flow status

#### Section 3: Operational Readiness (10 minutes)
- Financial statements availability
- Quality of bookkeeping
- Documented processes
- Management team depth
- Customer contracts
- Intellectual property documentation

#### Section 4: Market Position (5 minutes)
- Competitive advantages
- Market growth trends
- Customer retention
- Brand strength
- Scalability

### Completing the Assessment

1. Answer all questions honestly
   - The assessment is confidential
   - Accurate answers lead to better recommendations
   
2. Use the **"Save & Continue Later"** button if you need to pause

3. Click **"Submit Assessment"** when complete

### Understanding Your Results

After submission, you'll receive:

#### Overall Exit Readiness Score
- **80-100**: Excellent - Ready for market
- **60-79**: Good - Minor improvements needed
- **40-59**: Fair - Significant work required
- **0-39**: Poor - Major gaps to address

#### Category Scores
- **Financial Score**: Revenue quality, profitability, growth
- **Operational Score**: Systems, processes, team
- **Market Score**: Competitive position, growth potential

#### Valuation Estimates
- Estimated valuation range based on your financials
- Typical multiples for your industry
- How your score affects valuation

#### Recommendations
- Prioritized action items
- Quick wins vs. long-term improvements
- Suggested Missions to improve your score

> **💡 Tip**: Download your assessment report as a PDF to share with advisors or track progress over time.

## Business Profile

After your assessment, you'll be prompted to create your business profile (or it may be auto-created).

### What to Include

#### Basic Information
- **Business Name**: Your official company name
- **Legal Entity Name**: If different from business name
- **Industry**: Select from dropdown
- **Website**: Your company website URL

#### Contact Information
- **Business Address**: Physical location
- **Phone Number**: Main business line
- **Email**: General business email

#### Financial Overview
- **Annual Revenue**: Most recent full year
- **Number of Employees**: Full-time equivalents
- **Year Founded**: When the business started

> **Note**: This information is used to personalize your experience and provide relevant benchmarks. It's kept confidential.

## Choosing Your Path

Based on your exit timeline and goals, Legacy Architect OS recommends different priorities.

### Path 1: Immediate Exit (1-2 Years)

**Priority**: Get ready for market ASAP

**Focus Areas**:
1. ✅ Complete Data Room (Folders 0-10)
2. ✅ Document EBITDA add-backs
3. ✅ Address critical gaps in assessment
4. ✅ Prepare financial statements (3 years)
5. ✅ Execute quick-win Missions

**Recommended First Steps**:
- Start with **Buyer-Ready Data Room Mission**
- Upload existing financial documents
- Document your top 10 customers/contracts
- Identify and fix top 3 assessment gaps

### Path 2: Near-Term Exit (3-5 Years)

**Priority**: Optimize value while preparing

**Focus Areas**:
1. ✅ Improve Exit Readiness Score systematically
2. ✅ Execute revenue-boosting Missions
3. ✅ Build Data Room over time
4. ✅ Strengthen operational systems
5. ✅ Develop management team

**Recommended First Steps**:
- Start with **Cash-Boost Mission** to increase revenue
- Set up Data Room structure (don't need to complete immediately)
- Focus on improving lowest assessment category
- Track KPIs monthly

### Path 3: Long-Term Exit (5+ Years)

**Priority**: Build maximum value

**Focus Areas**:
1. ✅ Focus on revenue growth and profitability
2. ✅ Build scalable systems and processes
3. ✅ Reduce owner dependency
4. ✅ Strengthen competitive position
5. ✅ Document everything as you go

**Recommended First Steps**:
- Start with **24/7 AI Reception Mission** for lead generation
- Set up basic Data Room structure
- Establish quarterly assessment reviews
- Focus on strategic growth initiatives

### Not Sure? Take the Guided Path

If you're unsure which path to follow:

1. Click **"Guided Setup"** from the dashboard
2. Answer 3-5 questions about your goals and timeline
3. Get a personalized recommendation
4. Follow the step-by-step checklist

## Troubleshooting

### Can't log in?
- Verify your email first (check spam folder)
- Try password reset if you forgot your password
- Clear browser cache and try again
- Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)

### Assessment not saving?
- Check your internet connection
- Don't use browser back button during assessment
- Use "Save & Continue Later" button to save progress
- Contact support if issue persists

### Documents not uploading?
- Check file size (max 50MB per file)
- Supported formats: PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, PNG, JPG
- Ensure stable internet connection
- Try a different browser
