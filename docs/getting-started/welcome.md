# Welcome to Legacy Architect OS

**Retire Rich. Leave Clean.**

Legacy Architect OS is the comprehensive platform designed to help business owners maximize their exit value, prepare for acquisition, and execute a clean transition that protects their legacy.

## What is Legacy Architect OS?

Legacy Architect OS is an all-in-one business exit readiness platform that combines:

- **Exit Readiness Assessment** - Benchmark your business against acquisition standards
- **Buyer-Ready Data Room** - Organize critical documents using the industry-standard Folder 0-10 structure
- **Financial Optimization** - Document EBITDA, track KPIs, and prepare financials for due diligence
- **Revenue Acceleration** - Execute "Missions" to boost revenue and increase valuation
- **AI-Powered Insights** - Get actionable recommendations to improve your exit readiness
- **CRM & Lead Management** - Track opportunities and manage your sales pipeline

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Exit Readiness Assessment

Take a comprehensive assessment that evaluates your business across three critical dimensions:

- **Financial Readiness** - Revenue growth, margins, customer concentration, recurring revenue
- **Operational Readiness** - Documented processes, management team, systems, scalability
- **Market Readiness** - Competitive position, growth potential, industry trends

Get a scored report with:
- Overall Exit Readiness Score (0-100)
- Category breakdowns
- Valuation multiple estimates
- Actionable recommendations
- Comparison to industry benchmarks

### 2. Buyer-Ready Data Room

Organize your business documents using the proven Folder 0-10 structure that buyers expect:

- **Folder 0**: Executive Summary & Teaser
- **Folder 1**: Corporate Documents
- **Folder 2**: Financial Information
- **Folder 3**: Tax Information
- **Folder 4**: Operations
- **Folder 5**: Sales & Marketing
- **Folder 6**: Customers & Contracts
- **Folder 7**: Human Resources
- **Folder 8**: Technology & IP
- **Folder 9**: Legal & Compliance
- **Folder 10**: Real Estate & Assets

Features:
- Cloud storage integration (Google Drive, Dropbox)
- Version control
- Completion tracking
- Secure sharing with advisors
- Document templates and checklists

### 3. Financial Documentation

Prepare your financials for buyer scrutiny:

- **P&L and Balance Sheets** - Multi-year historical data
- **EBITDA Add-Backs** - Document legitimate adjustments with supporting evidence
- **KPI Tracking** - Monitor key metrics buyers care about
- **Working Capital** - Calculate and track normalized working capital
- **Contract Index** - Identify change-of-control clauses and risks

### 4. Missions (Revenue Acceleration)

Execute proven strategies to increase revenue and business value:

- **Cash-Boost Campaigns** - Generate immediate revenue from existing customers
- **24/7 AI Reception** - Capture leads around the clock
- **Reviews & Reputation** - Build social proof and credibility
- **Buyer-Ready Data Room** - Organize documents for due diligence

Track ROI and document results to show buyers your growth trajectory.

### 5. AI-Powered Business Audits

Get intelligent recommendations based on:
- Your industry and business model
- Exit timeline and goals
- Current readiness score
- Market conditions
- Best practices from successful exits

## Your Exit Journey

### Phase 1: Assessment (Week 1)
- Create your account
- Complete the Exit Readiness Assessment
- Review your score and recommendations
- Set your exit timeline and goals

### Phase 2: Foundation (Months 1-3)
- Set up your business profile
- Create your Data Room structure
- Upload critical documents
- Choose your first Mission

### Phase 3: Optimization (Months 3-12)
- Execute Missions to boost revenue
- Improve your Exit Readiness Score
- Complete your Data Room
- Document financials and add-backs
- Track KPIs and progress

### Phase 4: Preparation (Months 12-24)
- Retake assessments to show improvement
- Finalize all documentation
- Address remaining gaps
- Prepare for buyer conversations
- Engage M&A advisors

### Phase 5: Transaction (Months 24+)
- Share Data Room with qualified buyers
- Respond to RFIs (Requests for Information)
- Navigate due diligence
- Close the deal
- Transition successfully
