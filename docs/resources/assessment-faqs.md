# Exit Readiness Assessment FAQs

**Understand how your score is calculated and how to improve it.**

---

## Scoring Methodology

### How is the Exit Readiness Score calculated?
The score (0-100) is a weighted algorithm based on 4 pillars:
1. **Financials (35%)**: Historical performance, record keeping, and growth.
2. **Operations (25%)**: Reliance on owner, staff quality, and documentation.
3. **Market (20%)**: Competition, customer concentration, and growth potential.
4. **Legal/Risk (20%)**: IP protection, litigation, and compliance.

### What is a "Good" score?
- **80+ (Excellent)**: Strong candidate for a high-multiple exit. Deal likely to close fast.
- **60-79 (Good)**: Sellable, but may have some price haircuts during due diligence.
- **40-59 (Fair)**: Risky. Significant gaps need to be fixed before going to market.
- **<40 (Poor)**: Unlikely to sell. Focus on operational turnaround first.

### Does the score guarantee a sale price?
No. The score measures **readiness**, not value. However, a high readiness score correlates with receiving higher offers because it reduces buyer risk.

## Retaking the Assessment

### How often should I retake the assessment?
We recommend retaking it **every 90 days** or after you complete a significant Mission (e.g., implementing an SOP system).

### Will my old scores be saved?
Yes. You can view your historical progress in the **Assessment > History** tab. Seeing the trend line go up is great for team morale.

### Can I edit my previous answers?
Yes. When you click "Retake Assessment," your previous answers are pre-filled. You only need to change the answers for the things you've improved.

---

## Privacy & Sharing

### Who can see my score?
Only you and your invited Admins.

### Should I show my score to buyers?
**Generally, no.** The score is an internal tool for *you* to find weaknesses. Buyers will do their own diligence. However, showing a *trend* of improvement (e.g., "We improved our Ops score by 20 points this year") can be a powerful narrative.

## Improvement

### My score is stuck. What do I do?
Check the **Missions** tab. The system recommends specific actions based on your lowest-scoring categories. Prioritize the "High Impact" missions first.

### Why did my score go down?
Did you update your financials? If revenue declined or profit margins shrank, your Financials sub-score may have dropped. Honesty in the assessment is crucial for an accurate reading.
