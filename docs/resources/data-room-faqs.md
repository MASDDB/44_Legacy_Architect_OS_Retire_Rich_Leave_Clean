# Data Room FAQs

**Common questions about organizing, sharing, and securing your Data Room.**

---

## Documents & Storage

### What file types are supported?
We support over 25+ file formats including:
- **Office**: DOCX, XLSX, PPTX
- **Images**: JPG, PNG
- **PDF**: All standard PDFs

### Is there a storage limit?
- **Enterprise Plan**: Unlimited storage.
- **Growth Plan**: 50GB limit.
- **Starter Plan**: 10GB limit.

### Can I bulk upload files?
Yes. You can drag-and-drop entire folders from your desktop directly into the browser. Alternatively, use our [Dropbox](../how-to-guides/data-room/connect-dropbox.md) or [Google Drive](../how-to-guides/data-room/connect-google-drive.md) integrations.

## Access & Permissions

### What's the difference between "Viewer" and "Collaborator"?
- **Viewer**: Read-only access. Cannot delete or upload. Can trigger watermarks.
- **Collaborator**: Can upload and edit files in assigned folders. Good for your CPA or Lawyer.

### Can I hide specific folders from certain buyers?
Yes. This is called "Granular Permissions."
1. Right-click the folder.
2. Select **"Manage Permissions"**.
3. Set the specific user or group to **"No Access"**.

### How do I know if a buyer viewed my file?
Go to the **"Activity Log"** tab. You will see a real-time feed of every action: "John Doe viewed '2024 Financials.pdf' at 2:00 PM."

## Security

### What is Dynamic Watermarking?
When enabled, it stamps the viewer's email address and current timestamp across the document (e.g., "VIEWED BY john@buyer.com on DEC 08"). This prevents them from printing or sharing screenshots without tracing it back to them.

### Can I revoke access after sending an invite?
Yes. Go to **Users & Permissions**, find the user, and click **"Revoke Access"**. They will be immediately logged out and cannot return.

## Organization

### Can I rename folders after creating them?
Yes. Right-click the folder and select "Rename." *Note: This does not break existing links.*

### What is the "Staging" folder for?
The `_Staging` folder is a holding area visible only to Admins. Use it to review documents before dragging them into the live "Financials" or "Legal" folders that buyers can see.
