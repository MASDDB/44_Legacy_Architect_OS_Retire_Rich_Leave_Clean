# General Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Answers to common questions about your account, billing, and security.**

---

## Account Management

### How do I reset my password?
1. Go to the login page.
2. Click **"Forgot Password?"**.
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Check your email for a reset link (expires in 1 hour).

### Can I change my email address?
Yes. Go to **Settings > Profile** and enter your new email. You will need to verify the new address via a confirmation email before the change takes effect.

### How do I invite a new team member?
Admins can invite users by navigating to **Settings > Team > Invite Member**. See [How to Invite Team Members](../how-to-guides/account-management/invite-team-members.md) for details.

## Billing & Subscriptions

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and ACH bank transfers for annual Enterprise plans.

### How do I upgrade my plan?
You can upgrade at any time:
1. Go to **Settings > Billing**.
2. Click **"Change Plan"**.
3. Select your new tier.
*Prorated charges will differ based on where you are in your billing cycle.*

### Where can I find my invoices?
All past invoices are available for download in **Settings > Billing > Invoice History**.

### What happens if I cancel?
If you cancel, you will retain access until the end of your current billing period. After that, your Data Room will be locked, but your data will be preserved for 90 days before permanent deletion.

## Security & Privacy

### Is my data secure?
Yes. We use bank-level AES-256 encryption for all data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We are SOC 2 Type II compliant.

### Who can see my data?
Only you and the users you explicitly invite can access your Data Room. Legacy Architect OS support staff cannot access your documents without your written permission.

### Do you sell my data?
No. Your financial and operational data is yours alone. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

---

## Data Export

### Can I export my data?
Yes. As the account owner, you can export your entire Data Room structure and files at any time.

1. Go to **Data Room**.
2. Click the **"Actions"** menu (top right).
3. Select **"Export All"**.
4. You will receive a ZIP file containing your entire folder structure and documents.
